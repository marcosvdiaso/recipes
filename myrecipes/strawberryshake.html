Strawberry Banana High-Protein Shake

Description

This high-protein shake packed with omega-3 fatty acids can be enjoyed for breakfast or as an anytime meal replacement.

Ingredients

    1 ½ teaspoons flax seeds
    ½ cup soy milk
    ½ cup orange juice
    ½ banana
    4 strawberries, frozen
    1 scoop whey protein powder
    1 teaspoon flaxseed oil

Steps

  1. Place flax seeds in a food processor or coffee grinder; pulse until ground.
  2. Combine soy milk, orange juice, banana, strawberries, whey protein powder, ground flax seeds, and flax seed oil in a blender; blend until smooth.
